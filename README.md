### THE OPINION

Most teams are arguing about whether to use Tailwind 
or SCSS or CSS custom properties.

That's the wrong argument. They solve different problems.

- **SCSS partials** give you organizational structure
- **CSS custom properties** give you runtime flexibility  
- **Tailwind with a custom config** gives you 
  developer experience and repeatable patterns

Use all three deliberately. That's the architecture.
